Cruisin' the Caribbean

June 7th         June 8th           June 9th


June 7th, 2023

Today we arrived in the Caribbean island of Jamaica! It was a long flight but we made it!
I am ready to have some fun and swim in the ocean and go on a cruise! My family has been wanting to go on a cruise for a while!
When we got on the cruise, they had a pool and an all-you-can-eat buffet! It was so good!
They had a huge water park and a big recreation center with ping pong, darts, pickleball, and so much more! There was everything I could imagine!

  1. Ping Pong
  2. Darts
  3. Pickleball
IMAGE

June 8th, 2023

Today we decided to wake up and watch the sunrise. We had to wake up pretty early but it was worth it!
The sun was so pretty reflecting off the water, creating a picture perfect morning.
We then decided to go an swim in the lake ocean. It was very salty but not as cold as the other times I have been in the ocean.
After that we went and got some authentic caribbean cuisine and some tropical smoothies which was really good.
We had a great second day!

  • Sunrise
  • Swimming in the ocean
  • Authentic Food and Drink
IMAGE

June 9th, 2023

Today was super fun! We woke up and did an early morning kayaking tour! It was great! I hadn't been kayaking in forever!
After that we grabbed some lunch and then headed over to a ziplining tour!
We were up so high in the air! It was pretty and scary at the same time! My mom was too scared to go on so she stayed behind!
We then went back by the ocean to watch the pretty sunset. It is so relaxing at night and a good way to end our third day!
We also learned some Caribbean/Spanish words!

  • Chiquito - child

  • Jeva - woman

  • ¿Qué bolá? - what's up?
