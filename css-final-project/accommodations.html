BEST ACCOMMODATIONS IN CHICAGO

When you visit Chicago, there are plenty of accomodations to choose from. This list is to help you make the best choice.

1. Trump Hotel
The Trump Hotel has views from every spot in the hotel. This building stands at a monstrous 92 stories tall above North Michigan Avenue. There are restaurants, spas, and high-end ammenities all offered. However, all of this comes at a high price. But if you are looking for a high end stay, this is a great choice for you.
2. The Study at University of Chicago
This accommodation featrues a great fitness center, private parking, a garden, and a restaurant for all of the guests to enjoy. This 4-star hotel includes a bar. This hotel is a cheaper alternative to the Trump Hotel only starting at a little over $100.
3. Springhill Suites
Placed conviently in Chinatown, this hotel is close to many of the attractions Chicago has to offer. At the low cose of $98 a night, this hotel has everything you need for a great time in Chicago.
