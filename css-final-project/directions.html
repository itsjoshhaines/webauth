WHERE IS CHICAGO?

Chicago is located in the state of Illinois, in the United States of America. It is placed on the southwestern shore of Lake Michigan. It would take about 1 hours and 30 mintues to get to Milwaukee to Chicago. Chicago is also home to the O'hare International airport. Therefore, you can fly to Chicago from many different states and even many countries!
