BEST RESTAURANTS IN CHICAGO

There are a lot of restaurants to choose from. Here are our favorites:

1. The Capital Grille
This high class restaurant is a great American steakhouse. There are gluten free and vegetarian options available too! This restaurant has a 4.5 star rating on Trip Advisor and is ranked #3 of all the restaurants in Chicago. You won't regret going here.
2. Miss Ricky's
This restaurant is in a laid back setting. No more worrying about dressing all fancy! Miss Ricky's offers plenty of options for breakfast, brunch, lunch, and dinner. No matter the time of day you can always eat at Miss Ricky's!
3. Giordano's
You can't go to Chicago without trying their famous deep-dish pizza. And where else to try this but Giordano's! Known for their famous deep-dish pizza, Giordano's serves Italian food with plenty of options. Many have tried, but no restaurant's deep dish pizza compares to Giordano's!
